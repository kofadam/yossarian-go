🎯 What Does Yossarian Do?

Yossarian automatically finds and removes sensitive information from your log files, making them safe to share with external support teams, vendors, or less-secure storage.

🔋 What Gets Protected?

  • 🌐 Network Addresses (IP Addresses)
  • 👤 Usernames & Accounts
  • 🔑 Passwords & Credentials
  • 🎫 Security Tokens & Keys
  • 🏢 Your Custom Sensitive Words

📝 How to Use (3 Easy Steps)

  1. Add Your Words (Optional) - Go to "My Words" to add company-specific secrets
  2. Upload Files - Drag and drop or click to browse for files
  3. Download - Review results and download secured files
